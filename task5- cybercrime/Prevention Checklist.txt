Prevention Checklist:
* Use strong and unique passwords for all your online accounts 
* Never share OTP, UPI PIN, CVV or bank details with anyone 
* Do not click on unknown links or suspicious messages 
* Always verify payment requests before sending money 
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for important apps 
* Download apps only from trusted sources like Play Store or App Store 
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions 
* Regularly check bank statements and transaction alerts 
* Never approve collect requests from unknown persons 
* Do not scan random QR codes to receive money 
* Report online at National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in 
* Call Cyber Crime Helpline: ?? 1930
