PART B- Create a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist
DO’S
1. Post educational and positive content that reflects your interests and skills 
2. Maintain privacy settings to control who can see your personal information 
3. Use respectful language while commenting or messaging others 
4. Share achievements, projects and learning experiences 
5. Think before posting and ensure the content is appropriate and responsible

DON’TS
1. Do not share personal details like phone number, address or passwords 
2. Avoid posting offensive, negative or harmful comments 
3. Do not spread fake news or unverified information 
4. Avoid wasting too much time scrolling or getting distracted 
5. Do not upload content that can damage your digital reputation
